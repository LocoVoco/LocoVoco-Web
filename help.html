Getting Started

The app isn't available in the App Store as we are exclusively trialling it in Glasgow, you can download the app via Apple's official testing app, TestFlight. To install the app on your IPhone click on the following links:
First Install TestFlight
Then Install LocoVoco

When you sign in to the app it will ask for your location, for your privacy we don't store this information, it is only used to check your location is Glasgow (The trial area)
You login with your Apple ID, this is Apple's secure way to sign in with apps, this is a standard, secure way of logging into Apple apps.



Tour of Features

LocoVoco has 4 main areas of functionality Invites, Posts, Meetups and your User Profile

Invites

This is the place where you can browse through any invitations that other people have posted.
  • Swipe Left and Right to scroll through invites users have sent out, the invites you see here are also visible to other users.
  • The invites are ordererd by the date and time of the suggested meetup.
  • Accept an invite by adding a time and a greeting response.
  • Click Accept Invite, the user who posted will then either Confirm or Pass On the Meetup-Up
You can accept a maximum of 2 invites at any time

Post

Post out your own invites that other users will see, select a cafe, add a greeting and post!
  • Enter a friendly greeting that other users will see.
  • Pick a day and a range of times when you would like to meet.
  • Select the area of Glasgow you would like to meet up in South Side or West End
  • Select a cafe within that location
  • Post it!
  • Once a Post is created you can cancel it at any time
You can have a maximum of 2 posts out at any time
If you are arranging a meetup in the evening please check that the cafe selected is open at this time
The complete list of cafes with their location and website is available cafes
