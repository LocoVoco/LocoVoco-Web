Getting Started

The app isn't available in the App Store as we are exclusively trialing it in Glasgow, you can download LocoVoco via Apple's official testing app, TestFlight. To install the app on your iPhone click on the following links:
Step 1 - Install TestFlight
Step 2 - Install LocoVoco

When you sign in you'll be asked for your location, we don't store this information, it's a check that you're in the right meet-up area (Glasgow's the trial area)
You login with your Apple ID, this is Apple's secure way to sign in with apps, this is a standard, secure way of logging into Apple apps.



Tour of Features

LocoVoco has 4 main areas of functionality; Invites, Posts, Meetups and your User Profile

Invites

This is where you browse through the invitations that others have posted.
  • Swipe left or right to scroll through invites users have sent out, the invites you see here are also visible to all the other users.
  • The invites are ordered by the date and time of the suggested meet-up.
  • Accept an invite by adding a time and a greeting response.
  • Click Accept Invite, the user who posted will then either Confirm or Pass On the meet-up.
You can accept a maximum of 2 invites at any time

Post

Post your invites for others to see, select a cafe, add a greeting then post!
  • Enter a friendly greeting.
  • Select a day and a range of times when you can meet.
  • Set the area of Glasgow to meet up, either South Side or West End.
  • Choose a cafe.
  • Post it!
  • After you've created a post you can still cancel it at any time
You can have a maximum of 2 posts out at any one time.
If you're meeting up in the evening, please check first that the cafe you've chosen is still open.
The full list of cafes with their location and website is available at cafes

Meetups

Meetups are where you see the meet-ups you've got coming up. Either you've accepted a post, or someone's accepted one of yours.
  • After you've accepted a meet-up, the other person will either accept or pass on/ decline the meet-up.
  • When someone accepts your Post, you can either confirm or pass on the meet-up.

User Profile

Here you can edit your profile, change your photo, and add a blurb.
  • Add a profile picture, please add an appropriate photo, remember it's an app to meet friends üíõ not a dating app.
  • Add a good blurb about yourself, describe what you're into, other users see this on your invites.
  • Add your date of birth, users won't see this, they just see your age.
If you only want to meet other people of the same gender, that's no problem, you can select this in the Gender Invites section.
