Getting Started

The app is currently under review and will be released to the App Store soon.
When you sign in you'll be asked for your location, we don't store this information, it's a check that the app is available in your location.
You login with your Apple ID, this is Apple's secure way to sign in with apps.



Tour of Features

LocoVoco has 4 main areas of functionality; Profiles, Waves, Connects and your User Profile.

Profiles

This is where you browse through users profiles.
  • Swipe right through profiles of users, who are using the LocoVoco app.
  • If you like a profile then click the wave button to send them a wave.
  • The user will see your wave and either pass or accept it.

Waves

Here you see anyone who has seen your profile and sent you a wave.
  • View the users profile who has sent you a wave.
  • Pass on the wave, for the profile to be removed from your waves.
  • Accept the wave and you have a connect.

Connects

Here you see who has accepted your wave and who's waves you've accepted.
  • Chat to new Connects and make new friends.
  • If you feel you want to remove the user from your connects you can click the disconnect button.

User Profile

Here you can edit your profile, change your photo, and add a blurb.
  • Add a profile picture, please add an appropriate photo, remember it's an app to meet friends üíõ not a dating app.
  • Add a good blurb about yourself, describe what you're into.
If you only want to meet other people of a specific gender, that's no problem, you can select this in the profiles filter section.
