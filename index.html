Let's cure the disconnect.


The App

We are currently developing an App in Glasgow that allows users to meet like minded people for a coffee to have a chat see if they connect leading to meeting new people. It's a simple idea meet people in your local area for a coffee, don't chat to them on line all day, don't swipe left right to reject/accept people all day instead our view is if someone looks to be your type of person Then get out there and meet them for a coffee and see if you connect, why waste all day chatting via apps to see if you will connect with a new friend, meeting up with a person in the real world surely is a much more pleasant experience. Who doesn't like meeting new people in this cafe obsessed world
